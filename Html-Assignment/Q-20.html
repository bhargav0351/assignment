What are logical and physical tags in HTML?

    • 1.logical tag
    • logical tag means how display the text on webpage.
    • example, the strong tag is used to indicate that a piece of text is important,but the browser may choose to display it in bold, italics, or another way depending on the user's preferences.
    • 2.physical tag
    • Physical tags can be used to change the font, size, color, and other aspects of the text.
